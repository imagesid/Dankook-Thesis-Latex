\section{Results of Prestressing Load Application}

Function of the research results:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=3em, itemsep=2pt]
  \item To describe in detail the results obtained through the methods presented in the research methods section
  \item To support the research results by presenting evidence (data) directly through tables or figures
\end{itemize}

Contents included in the research results:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=3em, itemsep=2pt]
  \item Only results relevant to the research questions raised in the introduction should be presented
        (only data should be presented without interpretation --- interpretation should be included in the conclusion)
  \item All results should be included regardless of whether they support the research hypothesis
\end{itemize}

Verb tense in the research results: results should generally be reported in the past tense.
